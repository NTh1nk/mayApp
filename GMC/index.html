Coordinate Meetings Seamlessly

GMC – Global Meeting Coordinator – is your intelligent scheduling assistant. Say goodbye to back-and-forth emails and conflicting calendars. Simply input your availability and location preferences, and let GMC handle the rest.

  • COMT – Input your available time slots and locations of all participants. GMC will automatically compute the optimal meeting time across time zones.
  • COMP – Input the locations of your participants and your organization's headquarters. GMC will suggest the most convenient and equitable meeting location.

Whether you're coordinating across teams, departments, or continents – GMC simplifies the process and maximizes efficiency.
